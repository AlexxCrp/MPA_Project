How FinTrack Works

A simple, powerful approach to managing your finances in just a few steps.

1. Setup Your Accounts

Create virtual accounts that mirror your real-life financial structure. Add your current balances and account details to get started.

  • Connect multiple account types
  • Set initial balances
  • Categorize by purpose

2. Import Your Transactions

Upload your bank statements or add transactions manually. Our smart parsing technology automatically categorizes your spending.

  • Automatic statement parsing
  • Smart categorization
  • Easy manual entry option

3. Create Budget Plans

Set up personalized budget categories and spending limits. Plan for future expenses and track your progress over time.

  • Custom budget categories
  • Monthly spending limits
  • Future payment scheduling

4. Monitor Your Financial Health

Access detailed reports and visualizations that show your spending patterns, savings growth, and overall financial health.

  • Comprehensive dashboard
  • Visual spending analytics
  • Financial goal tracking
